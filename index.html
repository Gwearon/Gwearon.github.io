What is staking?

Staking or delegation is the process where ADA holders delegate the stake of their ADA to a staking pool. It enables ADA holders who do not have the skills or desire to run a full node to be part of the network and be rewarded proportionally based on the amount of staking that is delegated.

There is NO security risk for the user as you are not sending any funds to our pool, just delegating your account.
