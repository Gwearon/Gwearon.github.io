ADA Point Pool

Cardano stake pool that is focusing on security and cost optimizations

Why?

Our mission is to enable our users to securely delegate their ADA and get their staking rewards. With this in mind we will try to optimise other parameters to enable the most savings to be passed on to our users.

When?

Our staking node will go online as soon as IOHK releases a staking newtwork for the Cardano network.

Where?

Our cloud servers are currently in Germany.

Hardware?

Servers are virtualized and hardware only on a need basis. Therefore we can pass savings on to our users.
